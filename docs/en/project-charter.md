# 📋 Project Charter — AI & PM Research Hub Digital Platform

> Aligned with PMBOK® 8th Edition Performance Domains

## 1. Project Purpose & Justification

The AI & PM Research Hub requires a digital platform to support its transition from a regional Brazilian initiative to a globally recognized Center of Excellence and Community of Practice within the PMI ecosystem. The platform serves as the operational backbone for trilingual documentation, research output publication, cycle kickoff presentations, and the future AI Ambassadors program.

### Strategic Alignment (PMBOK 8 — Value Performance Domain)

| PMI Global Priority | Hub Contribution |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| PMI-CPMAI certification ecosystem | Research streams map to CPMAI methodology phases |
| AI in Project Management strategy | Thought leadership via 8 research streams |
| Chapter collaboration model | Multi-chapter blueprint (5 chapters, scalable) |
| M.O.R.E. framework adoption | AI-human collaboration research |

## 3. High-Level Scope

### In Scope
- GitHub repository with trilingual documentation
- Static website (Astro + Tailwind) with PMI brand standards
- Hosting on Cloudflare Pages (free tier)
- Change request log tracking Manual reforms
- Cycle 3 kickoff presentation integration
- Knowledge Base for research outputs
- i18n support: EN-US, PT-BR, ES-LATAM

### Out of Scope (for now)
- Member authentication / login system
- Dynamic database-driven features
- Payment processing
- Mobile native app
- Paid hosting infrastructure

## 5. Risks (PMBOK 8 — Uncertainty Performance Domain)

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| PMI Global launches AI Ambassadors independently | Medium | High | Accelerate international visibility and contact |
| Volunteer fatigue between cycles | Medium | Medium | Reduce friction, automate where possible |
| Brand compliance violation | Low | High | Audit all materials against PMI Policy Manual |
| Content obsolescence (fast-moving AI field) | High | Medium | Quarterly review cycle, living documents |
| Shadow AI in publications | Medium | High | Explicit LLM disclosure clause in Manual R3 |

## 6. Success Criteria

The project is considered successful when:
1. The Hub has a live, trilingual website accessible globally
2. All strategic analysis recommendations are logged as formal CRs
3. At least one international chapter has been contacted
4. Cycle 3 kickoff can be presented via the platform
5. The governance manual reform path is documented and communicated
