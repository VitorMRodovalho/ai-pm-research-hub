## CSRF Middleware
- `checkOrigin: false` in astro.config (Astro's check runs before middleware, blocks OAuth/MCP)
- Manual CSRF in `src/middleware.ts`: bypasses /oauth/, /mcp, /.well-known/; checks origin for all other POSTs
